GreenMind - Symbiotic Growth Initiative

Environmental and Personal Growth-Oriented Web Application for CSR Initiative

Hover Over the image to see image description

A team project focused on the ethical and academic growth of children while promoting environmental sustainability. Key features include:

  • Self-Growth Area: Offers cleansing content for ethical development, followed by quizzes that reward users with water points for correct answers.
  • Pomodoro Timer: Implements the Pomodoro Technique to enhance productivity, encouraging users to work in intervals of 25 minutes followed by 5-minute breaks, with rewards of sunlight points.
  • Meditation Spot: Provides meditation resources to foster a connection with humanity, rewarding users with soil points.
  • Progress Tracker: Displays the current Level and features a tree animation showing the conversion of a sapling to a tree as users progress, along with rain animation and Zen guidance from a cute panda character.

Upon completing five levels, the CSR team plants a tree on behalf of the user, reinforcing the application's commitment to environmental growth.


Contributions: Project idea, logo, splash designing and animation, some portion of website designing and progress tracker, self-growth area (both frontend and backend), Zen (Panda character) designing and animations, Zen pop-ups, and all animation work for the website (excluding login/register animations), including the navbar, board, cloud, rain, and tree growth animations.
